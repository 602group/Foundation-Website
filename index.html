About EPIC Foundation

We create once-in-a-lifetime golf experiences at the world's most exclusive courses while raising funds for charitable causes. Every auction supports our mission to make a meaningful impact through the game we love.

Exclusive Access

Gain entry to the most iconic and private courses in the world — from Augusta National and Pebble Beach to Pine Valley and beyond. These are courses that can't simply be booked online. Through EPIC Foundation, our members unlock doors that money alone can't open.

Charitable Impact

Every dollar raised through EPIC Foundation goes directly toward the causes we believe in. We support Folds of Honor, Hope Kids, and Youth on Course — organizations changing lives one round at a time. When you participate, you're not just playing golf; you're investing in something that truly matters.

Unforgettable Experiences

These aren't ordinary rounds of golf. Each EPIC experience is curated, member-hosted, and designed to be the round of a lifetime. Whether you're walking the same fairways as the legends or playing alongside a club member at one of the world's great courses, these are memories you'll carry forever.

Make a Difference

Support the Mission

Your contribution funds once-in-a-lifetime golf experiences for deserving youth, veterans, and communities that need it most. We partner with Folds of Honor, Hope Kids, and Youth on Course to make every dollar count. Every gift — no matter the size — creates real change.
