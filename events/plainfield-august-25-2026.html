Plainfield Country Club

Join us for a day at Plainfield Country Club on August 25th, 2026!

In the late 1990s, Gil Hanse began peeling back decades at Plainfield Country Club, restoring Donald Ross's original intent with a surgeon's touch. Through the early 2000s, greens, fairways, tees, and bunkers were brought back to life—and the overgrowth came down—revealing bold angles, cleaner lines, and long-lost vistas. The result is Ross at full volume: strategic, demanding, and absolutely pure, a championship canvas proven by winners like John Colt, Laura Davies, Dustin Johnson, and Jason Day.
