About EPIC Foundation

Creating once-in-a-lifetime golf experiences while making a lasting impact for the causes that matter most.

Our Mission

EPIC Foundation exists to democratize access to the world's most exclusive golf courses while generating meaningful support for charitable causes. We believe that unforgettable golf experiences shouldn't be limited to the privileged few, and that every round played can contribute to something greater than the game itself.

Through our innovative auction and event platform, we connect passionate golfers with once-in-a-lifetime opportunities at legendary courses—from Pebble Beach to Augusta National—while channeling 100% of proceeds toward organizations making real change in their communities.

Where Your Dollars Go

Our Three Charity Partners

Every auction, every donation, and every round played through EPIC Foundation directly benefits three organizations that are changing lives through the game of golf.

Military & First Responders

Folds of Honor

Folds of Honor provides educational scholarships to the spouses and children of America's fallen and disabled service members. EPIC Foundation is proud to direct proceeds toward this mission — honoring those who've sacrificed everything by investing in the futures of their families. Every round we auction helps fund a scholarship for a hero's child.

Learn More →
Children with Illness

Hope Kids

Hope Kids creates extraordinary experiences for children living with cancer and other life-threatening illnesses, and for their families. EPIC Foundation channels auction proceeds toward Hope Kids events — giving these remarkable families moments of joy, connection, and hope at some of the most difficult times of their lives. Golf becomes more than a game when it's a lifeline.

Learn More →
Youth Development

Youth on Course

Youth on Course is making golf accessible to young people across North America by offering rounds for $5 or less at hundreds of participating courses. EPIC Foundation supports this mission of inclusion — ensuring that the next generation of golfers isn't limited by financial barriers. Every dollar we raise opens a course gate for a kid who might not have had the chance otherwise.

Who We Are

EPIC Foundation was founded by a group of passionate golfers who saw an opportunity to bridge two worlds: the exclusive realm of private golf clubs and the broader community of players who dream of experiencing these legendary courses.

Our team combines decades of experience in golf, nonprofit management, and technology. We've built relationships with over 150 of the world's most prestigious clubs, from century-old American classics to hidden gems across Scotland and Ireland. These partnerships allow us to offer authentic, member-hosted experiences that simply aren't available anywhere else.

But we're more than just a platform for exclusive golf. We're a community of people who believe that privilege comes with responsibility, and that the joy of the game is amplified when it's shared and used for good.

Our Vision

We envision a future where golf is truly a game for everyone—where a kid from an inner city has the same chance to fall in love with the sport as one born into a country club family. Where veterans find healing and camaraderie on the course. Where golf courses lead the way in environmental stewardship.

By 2030, our goal is to have facilitated over $50 million in charitable giving while providing 10,000+ golfers with experiences they'll remember for a lifetime. We're expanding our network of partner courses globally, launching new programs that make golf more accessible, and pioneering innovative ways to measure and maximize our impact.

Every auction won, every event attended, every dollar donated brings us closer to this vision. When you participate in EPIC Foundation, you're not just playing golf—you're investing in the future of the game and the communities it touches.
