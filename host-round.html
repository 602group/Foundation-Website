Why Host a Round?

When you host a round through the EPIC Foundation, your contribution directly leads to real-world impact across three key areas.

Youth Development

Help introduce golf to underserved youth. Your round helps provide equipment, instruction, and access, ensuring the next generation learns life skills.

Veteran Support

Fund golf therapy programs for veterans. Golf offers therapeutic benefits for PTSD and rehabilitation, helping veterans connect and heal with dignity.

Conservation

Support sustainability initiatives. Contributions help protect natural habitats, conserve water, and promote eco-friendly maintenance.
